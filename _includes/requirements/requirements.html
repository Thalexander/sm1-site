What do we hope to accomplish?

The Microsoft Spot Market ChatBot will be a proof of concept bot that can be used by consumers to search for and be recommended products and deals without having to install a client app, you can connect by talking to the bot on your messaging platform of choice. The bot will provide customised recommendations through advanced machine learning techniques implemented by the Microsoft Spot Market Engine, which will over time learn what products you are interested in and if you grant it access to your social media accounts, it can better help you find the products you want.


To clearly define the goals of the Spot Market ChatBot we have created a list of requirements in MoSCoW format (Must have, Should have, Could have, Wont have)

Using the results of our research and development techniques, survey results and effective communication, we managed to gather the requirements Our main techinques include brainstorming, surveys, observation, mock-ups and prototyping


Must Have:

The bot must be interactive Functional
The bot must understand natural language Functional
The bot must use the Spot Market recommendation Functional
The bot must get the user's location if the channel of choice allows for it Functional
The bot must provide appropriate recommendations based on user's input Functional
The bot must recommend promotions and products in the vicinity of the user Functional
The bot must be able to link to the user profile Functional

Should Have:

The bot should be used to gather user feedback Functional
The bot should not require the installation of a client app Functional
The bot should be deployable on popular messaging platforms Functional
The bot should be able to narrow down and present the user the most appropriate suggestions based on the results returned by the recommendation engine Non-Functional

Could Have:

The bot could be energy efficient Non-Functional
The bot could be aesthetically pleasing Non-Functional
The bot could allow for discovery of new places when requested by the user Functional
